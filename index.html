When to Consider Surgery

Is It Time for Surgery?

Early intervention often leads to faster recovery and better outcomes. Consider consulting our specialists if you experience:

Persistent pain despite medications and therapy
Restricted mobility affecting daily activities
Severe arthritis or degenerative changes
Progressive nerve symptoms or structural deformities
